Taco

Description

Tacos are the best food in the planet. Nothing else in the world could contain this awe-inspiring combination of meat, lettuce, tomato - wait a second that's a burger.

  • Tortilla shell
  • Meat
  • Lettuce

  • Wait, is this a burger??

Steps

  1. Put meat into shell
  2. Put tomato and lettuce into taco
  3. Eat your fake burger vertically
